Calendar Productivity Tips

  • Color code your notes: Use emojis or tags for easy visual organization.
  • Review regularly: Check your notes and remove outdated events.
  • Plan ahead: Add notes for future dates to stay prepared!
  • Recurring notes: For weekly or monthly events, copy and paste notes to the corresponding dates.
  • Backup important notes: If you use another calendar app, regularly copy your notes to keep them safe.

Other Info

  • Privacy: Your notes are private and stored only in your browser.
  • Offline: Works without internet—access and update your notes any time.
