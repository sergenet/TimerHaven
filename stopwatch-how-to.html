How to Use Stopwatch

The Stopwatch tool is perfect for tracking elapsed time and split intervals. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start: Click Start to begin timing.
  2. Lap: Use Lap to record split times during your session.
  3. Pause: Click Pause to temporarily stop timing.
  4. Reset: Click Reset to clear the stopwatch and start fresh.

FAQs

  • Can I use the stopwatch for workouts? Yes, it’s great for exercise, runs, or intervals.
  • How do I record split times? Click Lap during timing.
  • Does it work offline? Yes, it works directly in your browser.
  • Is my data private? Yes, nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

Tips for Success

  • Use laps for intervals, sets, or splits.
  • Pause and resume when needed.
  • Reset between activities to keep timing accurate.
