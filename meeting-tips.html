Meeting Planning Tips

Make meetings more effective and stress-free with these practical tips:

Top Tips

  • Confirm time zones: Use the local time for each attendee when scheduling.
  • Share location or link: Include an address or online meeting link for easy joining.
  • Set reminders: Add calendar alerts or notifications so you never miss a meeting.
  • Use recurring meetings: For regular events, set up weekly or monthly recurrence to stay organized.
  • Edit meetings as plans change: Update details easily using the Edit feature.

Did You Know?

  • Meetings with a clear agenda tend to be shorter and more productive.
  • Recurring meetings can be managed within TimerHaven or in external calendars.
  • Your meetings are saved locally, so you can access them even after a refresh.
