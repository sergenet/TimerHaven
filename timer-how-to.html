How to Use Task Timer

The Task Timer tool is ideal for tracking any activity. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start: Click Start to begin timing your session.
  2. Pause: Click Pause to temporarily stop.
  3. Reset: Click Reset to clear and start fresh.
  4. Track: Use for work, study, breaks, or exercise.

FAQs

  • Can I use Task Timer for any activity? Yes—track work, study, breaks, exercise, and more.
  • Does it work offline? Yes, right in your browser.
  • Is my data private? Yes, nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

Tips for Success

  • Start fresh for every activity for accurate timing.
  • Use Task Timer for breaks to maintain focus and energy.
  • Track sessions to analyze your productivity.
