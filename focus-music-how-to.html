How to Use Focus Music

The Focus Music tool is designed to help you concentrate and stay productive. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Select a track: Pick a music style from Lo-fi Beats, Nature Sounds, or Ambient Piano.
  2. Play and pause: Use the player controls to start, pause, or stop the music at any time.
  3. Try different tracks: Switch between tracks to match your mood or energy.
  4. Repeat as needed: Replay your favorite track for longer focus sessions.

Tips for Better Focus

  • Use headphones to block out distractions.
  • Find your favorite music style for optimal concentration.
  • Keep the volume low to stay relaxed and alert.

Common Questions

  • Are tracks royalty-free? Yes, all music is free for demo and personal use.
  • Can I add my own music? Not yet—future versions may support custom uploads.
  • Will the music loop automatically? You can replay tracks manually as needed.
