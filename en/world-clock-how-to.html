How to Use World Clock

The World Clock tool helps you see the current time for cities around the world. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Add a city: Use the dropdown for popular cities or start typing any city name. Autocomplete helps you find the right spelling.
  2. Compare times: Add multiple cities to view their current local time side by side.
  3. Remove: Click "Remove" to delete a city from your list.
  4. Refresh: Times automatically update every minute for accuracy.

FAQs

  • How accurate is the time? Times come from GeoNames and update every minute.
  • Can I add any city? Yes, type any city name. Autocomplete helps you find the city fast.
  • Can I remove cities? Yes, just click "Remove" next to the city you want to delete.

Tips for Success

  • Use World Clock to plan meetings with people in different time zones.
  • Check local time before calling or travelling.
  • Bookmark this tool for instant access.
