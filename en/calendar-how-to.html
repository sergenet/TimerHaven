How to Use Calendar

The Calendar tool helps you track events, reminders, and notes for every day of the year. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Pick a month: Use the dropdown to select any month and year.
  2. Click a date: Click any day to open the notes modal for that date.
  3. Add multiple notes: Type in the box and press "Add Note"—each note is saved with a timestamp.
  4. View or delete notes: All notes for that day are shown in the modal. Click the trash icon to delete individual notes.
  5. Persistent storage: All notes are saved in your browser and remain until you remove them.

Tips for Organization

  • Add emojis or tags to your notes for quick visual sorting.
  • Review your notes regularly and remove ones you no longer need.
  • Plan ahead—add notes for upcoming events.
  • Bookmark the Calendar page for fast access!

Common Questions

  • Are my notes private? Yes, notes are stored locally in your browser.
  • Will my notes sync to other devices? Not yet—notes are per browser. For multi-device sync, use a cloud calendar.
  • Can I clear all notes? Delete notes for each day individually.
