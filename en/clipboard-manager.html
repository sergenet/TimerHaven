How to Use Clipboard Manager

  1. Add notes: Paste or type your note and click "Add Clip".
  2. Manage clips: Use the Copy or Remove buttons for each note.
  3. Clips are saved: They’ll remain even if you leave or reload the page.

FAQs
  • Are my notes saved? Yes! Notes are saved in your browser and remain until you clear them.
  • Can I copy a note? Yes, click the Copy button next to any note.
  • How do I remove a note? Click the Remove button next to it.
  • How do I clear all notes? Remove each note individually.

Clipboard Productivity Tips

  • Group notes: Keep similar info together for quick access.
  • Copy important info: Save time by storing commonly used text.
  • Remove old clips: Delete outdated notes regularly.
