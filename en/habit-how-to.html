How to Use Habit Tracker

The Habit Tracker tool helps you build positive routines and stay accountable. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Add a habit: Type your daily habit (e.g., "Drink Water") and click Add.
  2. Mark done: When you complete a habit for the day, click the Done button beside it.
  3. Remove habits: Delete habits you no longer want to track.
  4. Repeat daily: Return each day to update and track your progress.

Common Questions

  • Can I track multiple habits? Yes, add as many as you wish.
  • Will habits be saved? Habits are stored only for your current browser session.
  • Is there a streak feature? Not yet—future updates may support streaks and reminders.

Tips for Success

  • Start with easy habits and grow your list over time.
  • Use daily reminders to reinforce your new routines.
  • Celebrate small wins for motivation!
