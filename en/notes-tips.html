Notes Tips

Make your notes more useful with these practical tips:

Top Tips

  • Be concise: Short, clear notes are easier to review and act on.
  • Delete often: Keep your note list focused and relevant.
  • Use for tasks: Write quick to-dos, reminders, or ideas.
  • Organize: Use separate notes for different topics.

Did You Know?

  • Notes are not saved after refresh—use for quick thoughts.
  • You can add as many notes as you like.
