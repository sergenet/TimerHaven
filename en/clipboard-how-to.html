How to Use Clipboard Manager

The Clipboard Manager is a simple tool for saving, copying, and organizing multiple notes or snippets of text. Here’s how to make the most of it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Add a note: Type or paste any text into the box, then click Add Clip. Your note will appear in the list below.
  2. Copy a note: Click the Copy button next to any clip to instantly copy it to your system clipboard.
  3. Remove a note: Click Remove to delete any clip you no longer need.
  4. Clips are saved: All your clips are stored in your browser, so they stay even if you leave or reload the page. To clear all clips, remove them one by one.

Tips for Efficiency

  • Use the Clipboard Manager to collect research, drafts, or recurring text for fast access.
  • Group related notes together for easier organization.
  • Bookmark this tool for instant use any time.

Common Questions

  • Can I use this tool offline? Yes, it works entirely in your browser.
  • Are my notes private? Yes, clips are only stored on your device.
  • Will clips sync to other devices? Not yet—clips are saved per browser. For multi-device access, use cloud-based apps.
