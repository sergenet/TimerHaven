Habit Building Tips

Build habits that last with these practical tips:

Top Tips

  • Start small: Begin with easy, achievable habits to build momentum.
  • Track daily: Consistency is key—mark habits done every day.
  • Celebrate progress: Recognize small wins to stay motivated.

Did You Know?

  • Habits take 21+ days to become automatic—keep going!
  • Visual trackers boost accountability and make progress visible.
