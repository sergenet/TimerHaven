Calendar Productivity Tips

The Calendar can help you stay organized, plan ahead, and track important events. Here are top tips:

Tips

  • Color code your notes: Use emojis or text tags to visually group tasks or reminders.
  • Plan future events: Add notes to upcoming days for birthdays, deadlines, or goals.
  • Review regularly: Check your notes each week and clear out those you’ve completed.
  • Recurring notes: For weekly or monthly events, copy and paste notes to relevant dates.
  • Back up important info: If you use another calendar app, periodically copy your notes for safekeeping.

What Else?

  • Privacy: Your notes are private and saved only in your browser.
  • Offline: Works without internet—access and update notes anytime.
