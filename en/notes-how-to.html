How to Use Notes

The Notes tool is designed for quick thoughts, reminders, and lists. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Type your note: Enter your text in the box provided.
  2. Add note: Click Add Note to save it in your list.
  3. View notes: All your notes appear in the list below the form.
  4. Delete notes: Remove notes you no longer need using the delete button.

FAQs

  • Are notes saved after refresh? No, notes are for temporary browser use.
  • Can I add multiple notes? Yes, add as many as you wish.
  • Can I edit notes? Not yet—delete and re-add to update.

Tips for Success

  • Use short notes for quick ideas or to-dos.
  • Delete notes regularly to stay organized.
  • Use notes for reminders, lists, or important points.
