Stopwatch Tips

Maximize your accuracy and efficiency with these Stopwatch tips:

Top Tips

  • Use laps for intervals: Track split times in races, workouts, or sets.
  • Pause and resume: Useful for timing rest periods or interruptions.
  • Reset between activities: Start fresh for each session to keep records clear.
