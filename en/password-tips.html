Password Tips

Make your passwords stronger with these tips:

Top Tips

  • Use at least 12 characters: Longer passwords are harder to crack.
  • Include all character types: Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Never reuse passwords: Use a new password for every service.
  • Store securely: Use a dedicated password manager to keep track of your passwords.

Did You Know?

  • Password managers can generate and fill passwords for you automatically.
  • Changing your passwords regularly adds extra security.
