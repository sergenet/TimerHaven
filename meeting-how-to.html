How to Use Meeting Planner

The Meeting Planner tool helps you organize meetings across time zones. Here’s how to use it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter meeting details: Type a title, choose date and time, add a location or link, and select a recurrence option.
  2. Add meeting: Click Add Meeting to schedule it.
  3. Edit meetings: Click Edit next to a meeting to update its information.
  4. Delete meetings: Remove meetings you no longer need using the delete button.
  5. Persistent & recurring: Meetings are saved in your browser and can be set to repeat daily, weekly, or monthly.

FAQs

  • Can I edit meetings? Yes—click Edit to change meeting details.
  • Will meetings persist after refresh? Yes, meetings are saved in your browser (local).
  • Can I add recurring meetings? Yes—select recurrence when creating or editing.
  • Are meetings synced across devices? No, meetings are saved locally and will only show on this browser/device.

Tips for Success

  • Double-check time zones for all attendees.
  • Include links or addresses for easy access.
  • Set reminders outside TimerHaven to avoid missing meetings.
