Build a Productivity Routine

The Pomodoro technique helps you work in focused intervals with regular breaks. With TimerHaven, you can set your preferred work and break durations. Here’s how to build your routine:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. List your tasks for the day.
  2. Set your work and break durations in the Pomodoro Timer.
  3. Work for your chosen duration on a single task, with no distractions.
  4. Take a break for your chosen duration.
  5. Repeat: After four cycles, take a longer break to recharge.

Tips for Success

  • Use longer work periods for deep tasks, shorter for shallow work.
  • Customize breaks to fit your energy and schedule.
  • Track your completed Pomodoros for motivation.
